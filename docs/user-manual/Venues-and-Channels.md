# Venues and Channels

Canal4 organizes communications into **Venues** (spaces) and **Channels** (topics).

<a name="venues"></a>
## 🏢 Managing Venues

A Venue is the top-level container for all your displays, members, and messages.

### Creating a Venue
1.  On the main screen, click **"New Venue"**.
2.  Give it a descriptive name (e.g., "Grand Hotel Lobby").
3.  As the creator, you automatically become the **Owner** of this venue.

### Venue Settings
Access the **Venue Settings** from the kebab menu (⋮) inside a venue.
*   **Rename**: Update the display name of your venue.
*   **Danger Zone**: Delete the venue permanently. This will disconnect all display nodes and delete all messages/channels.

<a name="channels"></a>
## 💬 Understanding Channels

Channels allow you to target messages to specific displays or groups.

### Creating a Channel
1.  Inside a venue, open the menu (⋮) and select **"New Channel"**.
2.  Provide a name and an optional description.

### Channel Settings
Each channel has its own configuration:
*   **Minimum Role to View**: Restrict who can see this channel's history.
*   **Message Max Age**: Automatically hide/expire messages after a certain number of hours. This keeps the ticker relevant.
*   **Deletion**: Channels can be deleted by Owners to clean up old topics.

### Navigating Channels
When you click on a Venue, you'll see a list of all visible channels. Click on any channel to view the broadcast history or send a new message.
