# Getting Started

Canal4 uses a passwordless login system to keep your account secure and easy to access.

## 🔑 Your First Login

1.  **Enter your email**: Go to the Canal4 web dashboard and enter your work email address.
2.  **Receive your PIN**: Check your inbox for a 6-digit PIN.
    *   *Note: If you are running locally in development mode, the PIN will be printed in the server logs instead of being emailed.*
3.  **Verify**: Enter the 6-digit PIN on the login screen.
4.  **Complete Profile**: If this is your first time, you'll be asked for your name. This name will be visible to other members of your venues.

## 🧭 Navigating the Dashboard

Once logged in, you'll see your **Venues List**.
*   **Venues Card**: Shows all venues you are currently a member of.
*   **New Venue**: Button to create a new workspace (usually for owners/managers).
*   **Profile**: Access your account settings to change your name or delete your account.
*   **Logout**: Safely end your session.

## 🏠 Finding Your Way Home
You can always return to the main Venues list by clicking **"Venues"** in the top navigation bar.
